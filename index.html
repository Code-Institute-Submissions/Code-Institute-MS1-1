About

Who are we?

At Doug's, we believe choosing a healthy lifestyle should be an easy, straightforward decision. - we believe in growing and EVOLVING together, by supporting one another and creating a friendly and supportive community. We also believe that being healthy shouldn't be expensive and complicated - that's why we have a pay-as-you-go service.

Community

At Doug's, we're focused on growing and evolving together, so the community support its big for us - we're all about supporting members to reach their goals.

Fits your budget

Contrary to what people think, you don't need to flash a lot of cash to be a hero. At Doug's we accomodate many types of budgets - so you don't need to worry about money while you make a healthy choice.

No Contract!

Who needs them, anyway? With us at least, contracts don't exist. You simply sign-up with a credit card, and you're free to come and go and train as you please.
